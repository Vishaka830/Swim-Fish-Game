Instructions

  • The controls panel is present on the bottom right corner of the game window.
  • Click on the up arrow to move up, down arrow to move down, and right arrow in the middle to move straight.
  • You can also use the arrow keys of your keyboard (up, down, right)
  • Press the keys (up / down) multiple times to accelerate.
  • Try avoiding the obstacles using the control keys (pass through the holes). The game ends if you hit an obstacle.
